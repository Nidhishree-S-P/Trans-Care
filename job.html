Government Jobs and Reservations

While there is currently no separate policy under consideration for jobs to transgender persons, India has made strides in recognizing and advocating for the rights and inclusion of transgender individuals in various sectors. Efforts are being made to include transgender persons in existing reservation systems and to ensure fair employment opportunities within government jobs.
