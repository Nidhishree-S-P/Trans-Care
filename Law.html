Laws, Rights, and Sections for the Transgender Community in India

Constitutional Rights

  • Article 14: Right to Equality

    The Constitution guarantees the right to equality before the law and equal protection of the laws for all citizens, including transgender individuals.

  • Article 15: Prohibition of Discrimination

    The Constitution prohibits discrimination on the grounds of religion, race, caste, sex, or place of birth.

  • Article 16: Equality of Opportunity in Public Employment

    The Constitution ensures equality of opportunity for all citizens in matters relating to employment or appointment to any office under the State.

  • Article 19: Right to Freedom of Speech and Expression

    The Constitution guarantees the right to freedom of speech and expression, which includes the right to express one's gender identity.

  • Article 21: Right to Life and Personal Liberty

    The Constitution upholds the right to life and personal liberty, which includes the right to live with dignity and self-determination for transgender individuals.

Landmark Judgments

  • National Legal Services Authority vs. Union of India (2014)

    The Supreme Court recognized transgender people as a third gender, affirming their constitutional rights and freedom from discrimination.

Transgender Persons (Protection of Rights) Act, 2019

  • Section 3(b): Prohibition of Discrimination

    No person shall discriminate against a transgender person in any matter relating to employment or occupation.

  • Section 3(c): Right to Employment

    Every transgender person shall have the right to be employed without discrimination and should not be denied employment on the grounds of being a transgender person.

  • Section 10: Non-Discrimination in Employment

    Prohibits discrimination against transgender persons in employment matters, including recruitment, promotion, and other related issues.

  • Section 17: National Council for Transgender Persons

    Mandates the formation of a National Council for Transgender Persons to advise the government on policies and measures for the welfare of transgender individuals.
